# VoltGear Design System

## Brand Identity
VoltGear is a premium tech accessories brand. The design should convey trust, high quality, and modern aesthetics. It needs to be clean, professional, and eye-catching.

## Core Principles
1. **Minimalism with Impact**: Use ample whitespace to let products breathe.
2. **Glassmorphism & Depth**: Subtle shadows, blurred backgrounds, and layered components to create a sense of depth.
3. **Dynamic Interactivity**: Micro-interactions on hover, smooth transitions, and engaging scroll animations.
4. **Typographic Hierarchy**: Bold, clear headings contrasting with legible, understated body text.

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Indigo (`#4F46E5`) to Electric Violet (`#7C3AED`) gradients.
- **Secondary**: Emerald Green (`#10B981`) for success/badges.
- **Background**: 
  - Light mode: Off-white (`#F8FAFC`) with pure white (`#FFFFFF`) surface cards.
  - Dark mode: Very dark gray (`#0F172A`) with slate (`#1E293B`) surfaces.
- **Text**: 
  - Dark (`#0F172A`) for primary headings in light mode.
  - Slate (`#64748B`) for body text.

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Inter` or `Outfit`, bold, tight tracking.
- **Body**: `Inter`, regular, relaxed line-height.

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**: Pill-shaped or softly rounded rectangles with smooth hover lifts and gradient backgrounds.
- **Cards**: Borderless with soft diffusion shadows (`box-shadow: 0 10px 40px -10px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`).
- **Navigation**: Sticky, frosted glass effect (`backdrop-blur-md`).
